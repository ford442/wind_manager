Help — controls & physics

Quick start

Load a preset, watch the temperature overlay (blue = cooled sinking air), then try toggling latent heat OFF to see momentum-only updraft.

  • Click canvas — place water spray
  • Shift+click — air stream (momentum only)
  • Double-tap (touch) — air stream on empty canvas
  • Drag emitter — move; drag ring/handle — aim
  • Space / Burst — short high-rate spray on selected emitter (or all)
  • Share → Copy link — bookmark exact setup

Overlays

  • None — scene only (grass, trees, houses)
  • Velocity — speed magnitude + optional arrows
  • Temperature — warm spray / cold downdraft (default)
  • Humidity — moisture field; ground band shows accumulated deposition when enabled
  • Moisture — dedicated ground view: puddle + vapor stain map (persists after spray stops)
  • Droplets — Lagrangian spray particles
  • Flow tracers — passive markers riding the wind; enable streaks for paths

Latent heat

When ON, evaporating droplets cool the air (latent heat). Cooled air sinks via virtual-temperature buoyancy → downdraft and floor outflow. When OFF, spray momentum still pushes air up but no cooling → updraft.

Emitters

Water — droplets, humidity, evaporative cooling. Air stream — pure momentum, no mass or latent heat. Use numeric X/Y fields for exact positions (meters). Press Space or Burst for a short high-rate pulse — great with latent heat ON/OFF comparisons.
