wind_manager — evaporative downdraft

Two-way coupled air ⇄ droplet ⇄ temperature solver (WebGPU). A fine spray cools the air it rises through; the cooled column sinks and spreads along the floor as an outflow gust.

Hero experiment: with the latent heat toggle ON, the spray column turns cold (blue in the temperature overlay), sinks, and fans out along the floor. Turn it OFF and the same momentum-only spray drives an updraft instead — that flip is the proof the coupling is real physics, not a rendering artifact.
